About NYX Camping


With a passion for camping and music, Nyx was founded around two guiding principles: simple and affordable. After making our inception in Denver, Colorado, the team at Nyx has taken to the road, removing the hassle of festival camping and travel. From set up to tear down, we are on site to support our customer's camping needs throughout their festival stay.


Nyx is driven to provide our customers with simple and affordable options for festival camping. From the minute a customer reserves a tent, they join a community that provides top of the line support, giving them confidence to focus on the best part of music festivals - the music.
